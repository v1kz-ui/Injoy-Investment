Technology & Data Privacy

InjoyOS is our proprietary AI health platform that creates a digital twin of each resident's health trajectory. It ingests real-time biometric streams (ECG, SpO‚ÇÇ, HRV, sleep architecture, gait patterns) and uses ML models to predict health events days before symptoms appear ‚Äî UTIs 3-4 days early, falls days in advance, depression 1-2 weeks early. Unlike consumer wearables that provide information, InjoyOS provides intervention (clinical response). The platform is designed for B2B licensing to other senior care operators at 90%+ margins.

Data sovereignty is a core design principle. All health data is stored in DPDP Act 2023-compliant data vaults with end-to-end encryption, consent-based access controls, and complete audit trails. The system is ABDM (Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission) interoperable, allowing seamless data sharing with hospitals when authorized. Data never leaves India. Residents own their data and can request deletion at any time. We are building to CERT-In directive readiness for incident reporting compliance.

We partner with clinical-grade wearable manufacturers ‚Äî not consumer fitness trackers. These devices provide continuous monitoring of ECG, blood oxygen, heart rate variability, skin temperature, sleep stages, and gait analysis. The devices are unobtrusive, waterproof, and designed for 24/7 wear. All data streams into InjoyOS in real-time. Each villa also features ambient monitoring (motion, environmental sensors) and smart home integration ‚Äî all feeding into the resident's health dashboard accessible by family members through a secure app.
